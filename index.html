Valuation & Cash Flow

Present Value (PV) of future cash flows:

\[ PV = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \]

Where:

  • \( CF_t \): cash flow in period \( t \)
  • \( r \): discount rate / cost of equity
  • \( T \): projection horizon
This slide uses MathJax via the Reveal.js math plugin. Explain how we discount projected cash flows to evaluate business lines or portfolios, and how the discount rate reflects risk.

Analytics Snippet – CAGR Calculation

Example: computing revenue CAGR from Q3 FY 2021 to Q3 FY 2025.


def compute_cagr(begin_value, end_value, years):
    """
    Compute Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR).
    """
    return (end_value / begin_value) ** (1 / years) - 1

revenue_2021 = 8200   # ₹ Cr
revenue_2025 = 12300  # ₹ Cr
years = 4

cagr = compute_cagr(revenue_2021, revenue_2025, years)
print(f"CAGR: {cagr:.2%}")

Output example: CAGR: 10.28%
